Speedruns Rochester

RIT Representatives Scott "swc19" Csefai

Speedruns Rochester is an intercollegiate group of gamers within the Rochester area dedicated to beating games as quickly as possible. Join them on Saturdays from noon to 6pm in Gleason Hall Room 1149. They also host several speedrunning marathons each semester.

Contact: speedrunning@egsrit.com
